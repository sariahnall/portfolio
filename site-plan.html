Overview

Purpose

This website serves as a personal portfolio showcasing projects that reflect my skills, creativity, and passion. It shows my journey from academic learning to real-world application, demonstrating my commitment to growth in the tech field.

Audience

This site is designed for potential employers and fellow student who are intereested in exploring my work. I aim to make it easy for visitors to understand my capabilities in the field of software engineering.

Content

Home page

The Home Page introduces visitors to my personal portfolio, offering a glimpse into my journey as a software engineering student. It highlights my passion for technology and my commitment to growth, both in academics and through personal projects. The page serves as an overview of who I am and sets the tone for potential employers, or collaborators.

Images for the Home page

Projects

The Projects Page is dedicated to showcasing the practical applications of my skills through detailed descriptions of the projects I have worked on. Each project is categorized and includes information on the tools used, the problems solved, and the development process. This page highlights the range of projects I've worked on and demonstrates my growth as a developer.

Images for the Page 2

About Me

The About Me Page provides a deeper look into my background and goals in software engineering. It also serves as a way for visitors to get in touch with me, whether for collaboration, job opportunities, or to discuss ideas. This page includes contact information allowing professional networking. It emphasizes my openness to new opportunities and my eagerness to grow through collaboration.
